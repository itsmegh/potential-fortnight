Steps To Make a Sandwich

  1. Place two slices of your favorite bread on a plate
  2. Smear mayo and mustard on one side of each slice of bread
  3. Add three slices of deli turkey and one slice of cheese on top of one slice of bread
  4. On the bread slice without turkey and cheese, add pickle slices, tomato and arugula
  5. Put the sandwich together and enjoy!
